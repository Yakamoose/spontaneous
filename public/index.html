Here's the deal...

Modern Romance is a spontaneous flower delivery service. What that means is that you choose your flower boquet to deliver, and then choose the delivery window you want the flowers to be delivered in (2 weeks - 1 year). Then at a random time in your window your delivery will take place, thus making you the spontaneous and thoughtful person you are.
